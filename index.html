What makes PETRIX different

PETRIX Zero Sugar – Guilt‑Free Refreshment, Full‑On Flavour

Zero sugar. Zero compromise.
We stripped away every gram of added sugar and replaced it with nature's own boldness – sun‑ripened fruit, crisp botanicals, and a refreshing spark that hits exactly right. PETRIX Zero Sugar delivers all the mouth‑watering taste you crave without the sugar spike or the empty calories.

Naturally delicious, brilliantly clean.
Every can is a celebration of real fruit extracts and plant‑based sweetness. We never touch artificial sweeteners, synthetic colours, or preservatives. The result? A crystal‑clear, crisp taste that tastes like the fruit itself.

Your all‑day companion, redefined.
PETRIX Zero Sugar was made for the way you actually live. It's the cold can in your bag when the afternoon hits hard, the clink of ice on a hot patio, the perfectly light mixer for your evening wind‑down.

Why you'll love it:

🚫 0 g added sugar – pure fruit‑forward taste, no empty sweetness. 🌱 Naturally sweetened – only real fruit extracts & plant‑based magic. ✨ No artificial flavours, colours, or preservatives – what you taste is what nature intended. 💧 Light, crisp, and utterly refreshing – never syrupy or sticky. ♻️ Guilt‑free sipping – low‑calorie, vegan‑friendly, and packaged in infinitely recyclable cans.
